Open Window 64 Chart
This form takes the goal sheet to the next level. It is a specific framework that helps individuals develop 64 mini-tasks and routines to support the goal defined on the long-term goal sheet.

This chart consists of 81 boxes. At the center is the ultimate goal; it is surrounded by eight smaller tasks that the individual believes are required to support the central goal. Next, for each of those eight smaller tasks, identify eight even smaller goals.
